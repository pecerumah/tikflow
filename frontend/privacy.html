Section 01

Introduction

TikFlow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use TikFlow at flowtik.dimasgraft.workers.dev.

This policy applies to all users of TikFlow, including Super Admins and regular Users. By using TikFlow, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Our commitment: We believe in minimal data collection. We only collect what is strictly necessary to provide the TikFlow scheduling service — nothing more.
Section 02

We collect the following categories of information:

Category Data Points Purpose
Account Data Name, email address, hashed password Authentication & account management
TikTok Tokens OAuth access tokens, refresh tokens, TikTok Open ID, display name, avatar URL Posting videos to TikTok on your behalf

Section 03

How We Use Your Data

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery — Scheduling and posting your videos to TikTok at the specified times
  • Authentication — Verifying your identity and maintaining secure sessions
  • Account Management — Managing your profile, user roles, and TikTok account connections
  • Communication — Sending essential service notifications (e.g., failed posts, security alerts)
  • Security — Detecting and preventing unauthorized access or abuse
  • Improvement — Understanding how the Service is used to improve features

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose not listed above.

Section 04

TikTok Data & API Usage

TikFlow integrates with the TikTok API. When you connect your TikTok account, we receive and store:

  • Your TikTok Open ID (a unique anonymous identifier)
  • Display name and avatar (for UI display only)
  • OAuth access token and refresh token (for posting on your behalf)
  • Follower count and basic profile statistics
Token Security: TikTok tokens are stored in our Cloudflare D1 database and are used exclusively to post content you have scheduled. Tokens are automatically refreshed before expiry and invalidated when you disconnect your account.

TikFlow only requests the following TikTok API scopes:

  • user.info.basic — to display your profile information
  • video.upload — to upload your video to TikTok
  • video.publish — to publish your scheduled post

We access your TikTok data only when required to perform scheduling functions. TikTok's own Privacy Policy governs data held by TikTok.

Section 05

Data Storage & Location

TikFlow uses Cloudflare's infrastructure for all data storage:

  • Database (D1) — Account data, post metadata, and TikTok tokens stored in Cloudflare D1 (SQLite serverless). Data is replicated globally via Cloudflare's network.
  • Sessions (KV) — Login session tokens stored in Cloudflare KV with a 7-day expiry.
  • Video Files (R2) — Your videos are stored in your own personal Cloudflare R2 bucket. TikFlow does not control or retain your video files. You own and manage your R2 storage.

Cloudflare's data centers are located globally. For more information on Cloudflare's data handling, see Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Section 06

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • TikTok — We transmit your video content and post information to TikTok's API solely for the purpose of publishing your scheduled posts
  • Cloudflare — Our infrastructure provider processes data as described in Section 5
  • Legal Requirements — If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Safety — To protect the rights, property, or safety of TikFlow, our users, or the public
  • Business Transfer — In connection with a merger or acquisition (users will be notified)

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, analytics companies, or any other third parties.

Section 07

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using SHA-256 and never stored in plain text
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • TikTok tokens are stored server-side and never exposed to the browser
  • R2 credentials are stored encrypted in our database
  • Session tokens expire automatically and are invalidated on logout
  • Access is controlled by role-based permissions (Super Admin / User)
  • Audit logs track all sensitive actions for security review

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use a strong, unique password and report any suspected breach immediately.

Section 08

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service:

  • Account data — Retained until you delete your account
  • Post history — Retained for 12 months after posting, then automatically deleted
  • TikTok tokens — Deleted immediately when you disconnect the account
  • Session tokens — Expire after 7 days of inactivity
  • Audit logs — Retained for 90 days for security purposes
  • Video files — Stored in your own R2; TikFlow has no control over retention

Upon account deletion, all associated personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.
